Welcome to Young Efficient for Succes Community

There are currently around twenty of them benefiting from our digital programs within the YES association. Notebooks and pens are no longer enough to perform in one's field. From now on, phones, computers, and tablets are the main tools allowed during training. Children, young people, mothers looking for a better future, high school diploma or not, they all believe that digital technology can change their lives.

Today, all of our beneficiaries were right. They can increase their knowledge and follow intensive training with us thanks to digital technology. In addition, YES gradually forges the character of its young people. Throughout their training, autonomy, perseverance and rigor are the key words. Trainers have become facilitators of their learning and only monitor the progress of their apprentices.
